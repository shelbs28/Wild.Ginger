ABOUT WILD GINGER

Wild Ginger was opened in 2018 by Shelby Elrod. She sought to bring better sushi to the Franklin community. Her goal is to improve the lives of all through the use of perfectly made sushi. She intends to eventually open more franchises in the future and hopes to keep the culture of good sushi alive.
